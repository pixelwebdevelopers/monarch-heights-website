Monarch Heights is the tallest building in Dream Housing, Lahore. It is a 9-floor luxury residential and commercial tower developed by AA Monarch, featuring over 104,570 sq ft of covered area with 22+ premium amenities. It offers commercial outlets, hotel apartments, studio apartments, 1 & 2 bed apartments, and penthouses.

Monarch Heights is located at Plot No. 01 Tipu Block, Main Boulevard Dream Housing, Raiwind Road, Lahore, Pakistan. It is situated near Superior University and is just 5 minutes from Lahore Ring Road. The area is surrounded by 14+ universities, Bahria Orchard, and Lake City.

Monarch Heights offers 22+ premium amenities including a temperature-controlled swimming pool, modern gymnasium, rooftop garden with barbecue huts and family sitting areas, SPA & sauna bath facilities, Lahore's 1st floodlight sports area at 120 feet above ground level, and 24/7 CCTV monitoring and security for complete peace of mind.
